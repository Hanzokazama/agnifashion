About Us

Jewels are one of the most important things in the lives of women, and thus we are considering exploring this sector today.

Agni fashion opened it's doors in August of 2021 with a simple mission in mind: To provide high-quality, Handcrafted artisan jewellery and accessories at affordable prices that help support local and national charities.

Handmade jewelry can offer variety where factory pieces fall flat, especially when the typical offerings in brick and mortar, big box stores may not be the exact piece desired. From the earliest handmade jewellery of ancient times to today's artistic creations, the unique, one-of-a-kind pieces offered by jewelry artists can be a conversation piece that highlights any wardrobe.
